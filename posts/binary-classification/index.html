Classification

Classification refers to the process of categorizing objects into groups. Binary classification is a type of classification where objects are categorized into two groups.

The most popular algorithms to perform binary classification are:

  • Logistic Regression
  • Support Vector Machines
  • Decision Trees
  • Random Forests
  • Naive Bayes
  • K-Nearest Neighbors
